It's me, Mario!

I am a fictional character created by Japanese video game designer Shigeru Miyamoto. I am the the title character of the Mario video game franchise and the mascot of Japanese video game company Nintendo. I have appeared in over 200 video games since his creation. Depicted as a short, pudgy, Italian plumber who resides in the Mushroom Kingdom, his adventures generally center on rescuing Princess Peach from the Koopa villain Bowser. I have access to a variety of power-ups that give him different abilities. My fraternal twin brother and sidekick is Luigi. I first appeared as the player character of Donkey Kong (1981), a platform game.
